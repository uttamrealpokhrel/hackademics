About InfiniteQuiz

Our Mission

InfiniteQuiz is dedicated to making quality education accessible to everyone. We believe that learning should be engaging, interactive, and fun. Our AI-powered quiz generator creates unlimited multiple-choice quizzes on any topic, helping students and learners of all ages test and improve their knowledge.

What We Offer

  • ✅ Infinite Quiz Generation: Create unlimited quizzes on any topic using AI technology
  • ✅ Smart Gamification: Countdown timers add excitement and challenge to your learning
  • ✅ Instant Feedback: Get immediate feedback with detailed explanations for each answer
  • ✅ Progress Tracking: Monitor your performance with visual score displays and statistics
  • ✅ Mobile Friendly: Learn on any device - desktop, tablet, or mobile
  • ✅ Free to Use: No hidden charges, no subscriptions required

About Hackademics

Hackademics is an educational initiative powered by students and educators passionate about transforming learning. Founded by BSCHIT (Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Information Technology) students from AIMS College, we combine technology, innovation, and education.

Our Team

🎓 BScCSIT Students - AIMS College

A dedicated team of computer science students working to revolutionize online education through technology and innovation.


Vision: To provide accessible, quality education tools that make learning more engaging and effective.

Goal: To impact millions of learners worldwide through intelligent educational technology.

Technology Stack

InfiniteQuiz is built with modern, open-source technologies:

  • 💻 Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, Vanilla JavaScript
  • 🤖 AI Integration: OpenAI API with Structured JSON Output
  • ☁️ Architecture: Client-side processing for performance and privacy
  • 📱 Responsiveness: Fully responsive design for all devices
