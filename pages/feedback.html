Frequently Asked Questions

How will you use my feedback?

We carefully review all feedback and use it to prioritize improvements and develop new features. Your insights directly shape the future of InfiniteQuiz!

Will I receive a response?

If you check the "follow up" box and provide your email, we'll get back to you within 48 hours. For general feedback or suggestions, please use our contact form for more urgent matters.

Is my feedback private?

Yes, your feedback is confidential and will only be reviewed by our development team. Please check our Privacy Policy for more details.

Can I report a bug here?

Absolutely! Select "Bug Report" as your feedback type and provide as many details as possible about the issue. Screenshots are helpful too!
